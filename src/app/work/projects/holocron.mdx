## Overview

Holocron is a local-first knowledge base that combines the intuitive block editing of Notion with the tactile, industrial aesthetic of a Teenage Engineering synthesiser — without your notes living on someone else's server.

## The Problem

> "I love Notion, but I don't love the feeling that my brain is living on someone else's server."

Holocron resolves the tension between loving an intuitive note-taking tool and resenting the loss of data autonomy. It is privacy-native: offline-first, with your data staying on your machine.

## Key Features

- **Tactile block editor** — a `/` command palette and drag-and-drop
- **Industrial data grids** — relational data with status tracking
- **Local semantic search** — a Rust-based embedding engine that finds conceptual matches with no cloud indexing
- **AI co-processor** — an optional panel connecting to external models (via OpenRouter) with awareness of your database

## Technology

Svelte 5 and Tailwind CSS 4 on the front end; a Rust core packaged with Tauri for desktop; Yjs CRDTs plus IndexedDB for conflict-free offline persistence. Actively developed and already daily-use ready.
