## Overview

AssessFirst is a French HR-tech scale-up providing predictive recruitment solutions through advanced psychometric assessments. From September 2022 to June 2023, I led the UK market expansion, building the entire UK sales operation from scratch and establishing AssessFirst's presence in one of Europe's most competitive HR-tech markets.

## The Company

AssessFirst provides innovative talent assessment solutions that help companies:
- Make better hiring decisions through predictive analytics
- Reduce bias in recruitment processes
- Assess candidates' potential, not just past performance
- Improve quality of hire and reduce turnover
- Scale recruitment while maintaining quality standards

The platform uses scientifically validated psychometric assessments combined with AI to predict candidate success and cultural fit.

## The Challenge

AssessFirst had established success in France and was ready to expand to the UK market. However, the UK presented unique challenges:

### Market Entry Barriers
- Different HR regulatory environment
- Distinct recruitment practices and buyer behavior
- Competitive landscape with established UK players
- Need for localized sales and marketing approach

### Building from Zero
- No existing UK team or infrastructure
- Limited brand recognition in the market
- Need to establish credibility quickly
- Requirement to prove ROI in new market

## My Role: BDR/SDR Manager
