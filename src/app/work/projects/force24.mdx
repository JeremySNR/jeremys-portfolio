## Overview

Force24 is a Leeds-based marketing automation platform that helps businesses streamline their digital marketing efforts through intelligent automation, email marketing, and customer engagement tools. During my tenure from October 2020 to September 2022, I rapidly advanced through four roles in under two years, ultimately leading the Inside Sales team.

## The Company

Force24 provides comprehensive marketing automation solutions for businesses looking to:
- Automate email marketing campaigns
- Track and nurture leads through the sales funnel
- Integrate marketing and sales activities
- Measure and optimize campaign performance
- Scale marketing operations efficiently
